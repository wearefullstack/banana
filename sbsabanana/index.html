A view of the future today

How to use this system

Since December 2015, we've been working on a system which brings together our Standard Bank data along with non-traditional data services within the business. The system that has been developed today leverages our internal skill sets, along with those of our partners to create a dynamic and powerful solution for the bank as a whole, and presents us with a new future that soon be arriving which bridges the world of traditional data insights, and more data in motion insights.

The power of partnership

The solution brought together the Standard Bank Big Data team, along with local Hortonworks partners Full Stack, to deliver a solution which includes all of the major social networks, along with major location reputational player, Hello Peter.
